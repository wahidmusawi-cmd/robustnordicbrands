Who we are

A Nordic house of brands

Robust Nordic combines researched raw materials, considered formulations and Nordic quality. Under one roof we bring together four complementary brands — Robust Nordic supplements, DailyVital, Aurora Skincare and Aurora Haircare.

We believe in science: we select ingredients based on research evidence and use them in amounts that make a difference, with no unnecessary fillers, binders or artificial additives. Every batch is fully documented and traceable.
